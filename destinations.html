Destinations:

Until year.2200 you can explore these Planets with us

  • Proxima Centauri -- The closest known star to the Solar System, part of the Alpha Centauri system, which includes potentially habitable exoplanets.
  • Trappist-1 -- A star system with several Earth-sized exoplanets in the habitable zone, making it an intriguing target for future exploration.
  • Alpha Centauri A&B -- Twin stars in the Alpha Centauri system that are relatively close and might have potentially habitable planets.
  • Bernard's Star -- A red dwarf star with a confirmed exoplanet, Barnard's Star b, which is one of the closest known exoplanets to Earth.
  • Luhman 16 -- A binary brown dwarf system known as "WISE 1049-5319," with potential for studying the atmospheres of brown dwarfs.
  • Ross 128 -- A red dwarf star with a potentially habitable exoplanet, Ross 128 b, located about 11 light-years away.
  • Tau Ceti e -- A Sun-like star known to have a debris disk and several exoplanets within its habitable zone.
  • Gliese 581 c -- A red dwarf star known to have several exoplanets, including Gliese 581 c, which was considered a candidate for potential habitability.
